Rose-Hulman RISE Club

RISE is an undergraduate technical entrepreneurship club at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology. All of the members in RISE are Rose-Hulman students and allumni and are engineerers and scientist

RISE's Mission

The mission of the Rose-Hulman Innovative Student Entrepreneurship (RISE) club is to foster an entrepreneurial mindset that empowers students to develop innovative solutions, lead with integrity, and drive meaningful change in a rapidly evolving world. We aim to cultivate a collaborative environment that nurtures creativity, equips students with the skills and resources to succeed, and inspires them to become principled leaders in their respective fields.
